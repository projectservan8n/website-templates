OUR STORY

BORN FROM FIRE,
RAISED IN ZAMBOANGA

What started as an online kitchen in 2022 quickly became Zamboanga City's go-to for bold, fiery chicken. Hot Chicks brings Asian-American fusion to the table with dishes that pack serious heat and even bigger flavor.

Our promise: the freshest and highest quality food and all sides made fresh in-house daily. From our signature Nashville Hot Chicken Sandwich to our loaded fries drowning in cheese and jalapeños — every dish is built to be unforgettable.

FREQUENTLY ASKED

Monday – Saturday: 11:00 AM – 8:00 PM
Sunday: Closed

Last order cut-off: 7:15 PM for dine-in, 7:30 PM for takeout & delivery orders.
Ground Floor, QNS Building, Veterans Ave Ext. — near Al-Jaharah Village & HK1 Apartment, Zamboanga City.
Cash and GCash accepted in-store only. We do not accept debit or credit card payments at the moment.
We do not accept advance orders, pick-up requests, or orders made through Facebook Messenger or any unofficial channels. Walk-in and delivery only.
We deliver through Maxim only. Select "Pabili" and pin "Hot Chicks ZC". Delivery orders can be placed starting at 11:00 AM when the store opens.

We are not yet available on Food Panda or Grab Food.
We do not accept table reservations. Seating is strictly first-come, first-serve to keep service fair and fast for all guests.
A ₱50 corkage fee applies per beverage/bottle and per outside food item. Birthday cakes are allowed with no corkage charge.
