Our Story

We Keep
It Real

Born from the streets of Zamboanga, Pick Pocket started as a simple idea: give the city the bold, no-frills street food it deserves. Every skewer is grilled over real charcoal. Every rice meal is packed with love and zero shortcuts.

Tucked along MCLL Highway in Brgy. Boalan, we’ve become the go-to spot for locals who know that good food doesn’t need to be fancy — it just needs to be real. Over 16,000 fans can’t be wrong.
