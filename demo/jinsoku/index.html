Our Story

A Taste of Japan in Zamboanga

Jinsoku (迅速) means "swift" in Japanese—reflecting our commitment to serving authentic, soul-warming Japanese cuisine without the wait. From rich ramen and hearty donburi to crispy tempura and specialty dishes, every item is crafted with care.

Located at L'Arcada Complex, we bring the art of Japanese cooking to Zamboanga City. Whether you're craving noodles, rice bowls, or our chef's specials, each dish is a journey to the heart of Japan.
