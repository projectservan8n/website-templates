## The Problems Seville Faces

### 1. They're a Hidden Gem
- THE VANTA location is new, not everyone knows where it is
- People searching "café Zamboanga" or "coffee shop near me" won't find them easily
- A website with proper Google indexing changes that

### 2. Multiple Revenue Streams = Hard to Communicate
- They're a café AND a community/event space AND do prenup shoots
- Hard to show all of that on a Facebook page
- A website can have dedicated sections: Menu | Events | Book the Space

### 3. Event/Booking Inquiries
- Every prenup or event inquiry requires back-and-forth on Messenger
- "What's included? How much? What time slots?"
- A website can answer all of this upfront, filtering serious inquiries

### 4. Brand Consistency
- They have a strong 70s retro aesthetic
- Instagram captures it, but it's fragmented across posts
- A website is ONE cohesive showcase of their brand
