## The Problems Jinsoku Faces

### 1. Discoverability
- When someone searches "Japanese restaurant Zamboanga" or "best ramen Zamboanga City", does Jinsoku show up?
- Competitors WITH websites rank higher on Google
- A Facebook page alone doesn't compete with a proper website for search

### 2. Menu Access
- Customers have to scroll through Facebook posts or message to ask for menu
- A website puts the full menu one click away, 24/7
- Less "how much is the katsu curry?" messages = less staff time wasted

### 3. Credibility
- L'Arcada has multiple food spots - Jinsoku needs to stand out
- A professional website says "this place is serious about their food"
- First-time visitors can preview the vibe before coming

## The ROI Case

```
Average spend per customer: ₱250-400
Monthly maintenance: ₱1,200

Break-even: 3-4 extra customers per month

If website brings 10 new customers/month:
→ ₱2,500-4,000 extra monthly revenue
→ That's 2-3x return on the maintenance cost
```

**The question:** "If your website brought just 4 extra customers a month, would ₱1,200 be worth it?"
