Our Story

Born from a
Love of Filipino
Desserts

Manolo’s Halo-Halo started with one dream: bring the full joy of Filipino dessert culture to every corner of Zamboanga City. What began as a passion project has grown into one of the city’s most-loved dessert stops, with over 14,000 fans cheering us on.

Every cup of halo-halo we serve is packed with house-made leche flan, creamy ube ice cream, coconut strips, sweet beans, and a mountain of shaved ice. We don’t cut corners — we just pile it higher.
