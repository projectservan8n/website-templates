Our Story

Nyla's
Cafe

Nyla's Cafe by Cats & Cafe Milk Tea & Resto Hub is located along Don Toribio Street, Tetuan, Zamboanga City. We serve milk tea, coffee, frappes, rice meals, shawarma, Malaysian food, pizza, pasta, waffles, and traditional Tausug delicacies (Bangbang Sug).

Open daily from 10AM to 12AM. Available for delivery via Maxim and Food Panda.

Coffee & Frappes Lattes, frappes, fruit shakes, and non-coffee blends
Open Daily 10AM – 12AM Monday to Sunday
Milk Tea Bar Over 15 flavors of cheesecake milk tea in all sizes
Food Menu Rice meals, shawarma, pasta, pizza, Malaysian food & more
Delivery Available Order via Maxim or Food Panda for delivery to your door
Tausug Delicacies Authentic Bangbang Sug treats — a taste of heritage
Tetuan, ZC Zamboanga City
