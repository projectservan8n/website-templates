Our Story

A Passion for
Timeless Beauty

Founded in 2012, Bella Vida Salon & Spa has been Zamboanga City's premier destination for beauty and wellness. Our name, meaning "Beautiful Life" in Spanish, reflects our mission to help every client feel confident and radiant in their own skin.

Our team of skilled professionals combines international training with local expertise, using only premium products to deliver exceptional results. From classic cuts to trending styles, from relaxing spa treatments to stunning nail art, we create personalized experiences that leave you looking and feeling your absolute best.
