Requirements

Please prepare the following for enrollment:

  • Recent report card or transcript of records
  • 2x2 ID photo (for registration card)
  • Valid ID of parent/guardian
  • Registration fee (P500, deductible from tuition)
  • Completed diagnostic assessment (on-site)

Payment Methods

We accept the following payment options:

  • Cash (at our center)
  • GCash / Maya
  • Bank Transfer (BDO, BPI, Landbank)
  • Post-dated checks

Installment Plans Available!
Split your payment into 2-3 installments with no additional fees. Ask our staff for details.
