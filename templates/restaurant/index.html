Our Story

A Legacy of
Authentic Flavors

For over 29 years, Mariscos del Sur Seafood House has been serving the finest seafood dishes in Zamboanga City. Our journey began in 1995 with a simple vision: to share the incredible bounty of our local waters with family and friends.

Today, we continue that tradition with the same passion and commitment to quality. Every dish is prepared using the freshest ingredients, sourced daily from local fishermen who share our dedication to excellence.
