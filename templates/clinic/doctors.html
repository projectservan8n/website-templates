About

Dr. Maria Isabel Santos is a board-certified dermatologist with over 15 years of experience in both medical and cosmetic dermatology. She is passionate about helping patients achieve healthy, beautiful skin through evidence-based treatments and believes in empowering patients through education about proper skincare.
