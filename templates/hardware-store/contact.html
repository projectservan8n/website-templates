FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Common questions about orders and delivery

Do you offer contractor discounts?

Yes! We offer special pricing for contractors and bulk orders. The more you buy, the better the price. Contact us with your requirements for a custom quote.

What is the minimum order for delivery?

Minimum order for free delivery within Zamboanga City is ₱5,000. Smaller orders can be delivered for a fee. Contact us for specific rates.

Can you source items not in your inventory?

Absolutely! We have connections with multiple suppliers. If you need something specific, let us know and we'll try to source it for you at competitive prices.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, bank transfer, GCash, and major credit cards. For large orders, we can discuss payment terms. Terms are available for qualified contractors.
