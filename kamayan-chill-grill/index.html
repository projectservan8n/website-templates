Our Story

More Than Just a Meal

Kamayan Chill & Grill is Zamboanga City's go-to bar and grill for authentic Filipino flavors, cold drinks, and a laid-back vibe. Whether you're here for a quick lunch, a night out with friends, or a full catering event — we've got you covered.

Located along Phoenix Veterans Avenue, we bring the warmth of kamayan-style dining with the energy of a chill grill spot. Chicken inasal, grilled favorites, and ice-cold beers — always served fresh.
